Frequently Asked Questions

What is the right age to start Waseem Infant Formula? +
Stage 1 is suitable from birth to 3 months. Always consult your pediatrician before starting formula.
How do I prepare the milk correctly? +
Boil water and cool to 40°C. Add exact scoops as per pack instructions. Never use microwave.
Can I switch from breastfeeding? +
Yes. We recommend gradual transition over 7–10 days for best results.
How long can I store an opened tin/pack? +
Use within 3 weeks after opening. Store in a cool, dry place away from sunlight.
